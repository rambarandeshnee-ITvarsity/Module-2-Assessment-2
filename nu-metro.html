Nu Metro has been one of South Africa’s leading cinema chains for over 30 years – currently operating 24 cineplexes with a national footprint in Cape Town, Port Elizabeth, Durban, Amanzimtoti, Johannesburg, Krugersdorp, Pretoria, Bloemfontein and Worcester. New cinemas have opened in Welkom, Lenasia, Ballito and Pretoria East, Umhlanga, Ballito, Maputo, Lusaka, Mamelodi, and we’re coming soon to Giyani. Nu Metro strives to offer consumers an exceptional and unmatched cinema experience through: regular talent visits by movie stars; competitions, promotions and activations; popular pre-release screenings and partnerships with some of South Africa’s leading brands; combined with the highest level of personalised service and the very best in sound and projection technology.
